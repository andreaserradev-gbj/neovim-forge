## TODO Navigation on the Corne

| Operation | Keys | Hand |
|---|---|---|
| `]t` (next) | `]` + `t` | Right + right |
| `[t` (prev) | `[` + `t` | Right + left |
| `<leader>xt` | Space + x + t | Left thumb + right side |

All comfortable sequences. Nothing requires special modifiers or stretching.

---

## Custom Keymaps Philosophy

By Day 26, you understand your workflow. Custom keymaps automate repetitive tasks:

- Frequently-used commands
- Project-specific workflows
- Personal preferences

The Corne's consistent layout makes custom keymaps work well because you understand the physical positions. If you create `<leader>c` for something, you know `c` is on the left side.

---

## Workflow Integration

TODOs + custom keymaps = faster iteration:

1. As you code, drop `TODO` comments
2. Use custom macros to jump to them quickly
3. Track progress with the TODO list
4. Refactor without losing context

This is the essence of keyboard-driven development: every tool you need is one keystroke away.
