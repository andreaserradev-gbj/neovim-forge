## Exercise 1: Session Management
Save a Vim session with `<leader>qq`. Close Vim. Reopen and restore the session.

## Exercise 2: UI Toggles
Try each UI toggle:
- `<leader>uz` for zen mode (perfect for presentations)
- `<leader>uw` to toggle word wrap
- `<leader>ul` to toggle line numbers

## Exercise 3: Deploy
Deploy your site to Vercel or Netlify. Share the link.

## Exercise 4: Reflection
What's your workflow for presentations? How do zen mode and UI toggles help?
